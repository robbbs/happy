Consent

Please consider this information carefully before signing up for an account and participating in this study.


Purpose of the research:

We are a group of scientists, affiliated with Cornell University and the University of Michigan, gathering data to develop an index of subjective well-being. If you have any questions about the research or your participation in this study, feel free to contact us: Professors Daniel Benjamin, Ori Heffetz, Miles Kimball


Risks:

No risks are anticipated.


Confidentiality:

All research data will be collected confidentially without any personally-identifiable information. If you choose to sign up for an account, we will store your email address separately from your confidential survey response data and only you will be able to login and access your data. When you sign in or respond to our survey all information will be transmitted over an encrypted SSL connection, the same level of security that protects your username and password when you sign on to Gmail or Amazon.com.


Participation and withdrawal:

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. At any time you may choose to terminate your user account and delete your account information.
