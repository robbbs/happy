Consent

Please consider this information carefully before deciding whether to participate in this research.


Purpose of the research:

We are group of scientists, affiliated with Cornell University and the University of Michigan, gathering data to develop an index of subjective well-being.


What we will ask you about:

We will ask you a series of questions, first about the level of various aspects of your well-being and then about the relative importance of each of these aspects in your life.


Risks:

No risks are anticipated.


Confidentiality:

All research data will be collected annonymously and we will not store any personally-identifiable information. If you choose to sign up for an account, we will store your email address separately from your anonymized survey response data and only you will be able to login and access your data.


Participation and withdrawal:

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. At any time you may choose to terminate your user account and delete your survey response data.
